Introduction

Hello and welcome to ZeedTest! Your privacy is super important to us, and we want you to feel confident about how we handle your information. Here’s everything you need to know about what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information

Language Preference: We let you choose your language preference in the app. That’s the only personal info we collect, and it helps us improve your experience!

Usage Data

As you navigate through the app, we automatically gather some technical information, including:

  • Your IP address
  • Type and version of your browser
  • Operating system details
  • Pages you visit and how long you stay on them

Speed Test Data

When you run a speed test, we collect:

  • Download and upload speeds
  • The date and time of each test

This data helps you track your internet performance over time!

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information for several reasons, including:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services: Your data helps us run the speed tests and understand how our app is being used.
  • To Enhance User Experience: By analyzing usage data, we can identify areas for improvement and make ZeedTest even better for you.
  • To Communicate with You: If you reach out with questions or feedback, we’ll use your info to get back to you.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We’re keeping it simple: we don’t use cookies or any tracking technologies in ZeedTest. This means no invasive tracking—just a straightforward app experience!

How Long Do We Keep Your Data?

We retain your speed test results until you decide to clear them. You can manage your speed test history at any time through the app settings. Just one click and it's gone!

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your information, including:

  • Access: You can request to see the information we have about you.
  • Correction: If something’s off, you can ask us to correct it.
  • Deletion: You’re in control! You can clear your speed test history whenever you want.

Security of Your Information

We take your security seriously and use reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no online platform can guarantee complete security. We’re doing our best to keep your data safe!

Sharing Your Information

Rest assured, we don’t sell or trade your personal information with third parties. We may share your data with trusted service providers who help us operate the app, but they are bound to keep your information confidential.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we’ll let you know by updating the date at the top and possibly through a notification in the app.
